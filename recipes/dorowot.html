Steps

  1. Wash and soak the chicken in cold water with lemon squeezed into it for 30 minutes. (This removes any bacteria and tenderizes the meat.)
  2. aramelize the red onions on low heat in a large pot for 1 hour.
  3. Add the berbere, Niter Kibbeh, garlic and ginger
  4. cook for 30 minutes on medium-low heat.
